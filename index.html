About the School

We’re a Zagreb-based theatre school where English is the language of play. Every class blends acting games, movement, and short scenes so children absorb vocabulary and grammar naturally—by using them.

Beginners are welcome. Groups are small and level-based for ages 6–16.

What Parents Love

Our Method

  1. Warm-up in English — voice, rhythm, and fun articulation drills.
  2. Games & improv — vocabulary becomes action and reaction.
  3. Short scripts — memorising lines builds accuracy and confidence.
  4. Showcase — families see progress in friendly term-end performances.

What They Learn

  • Pronunciation, stress, and intonation
  • Useful vocabulary & functional phrases
  • Teamwork, stage presence, and public speaking
  • Creative problem-solving—entirely in English
